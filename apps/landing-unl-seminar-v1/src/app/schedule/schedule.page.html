The programme will highlight the work and recent developments in the field of heterogeneous catalysis through the two plenary conferences, the distinguished keynote lecturers (25 min + 5 min discussion) and will be complemented by oral presentations (15 min + 5 min discussion) and flash poster presentations (3 min online only).

The information related to the activities the workshop can be consulted by clicking/tapping on them in the programme list. The activities include description and bios of speakers for Conferences and Keynotes, the abstracts for Presentations, and finally the videos for each presentation of the Poster Sessions.
