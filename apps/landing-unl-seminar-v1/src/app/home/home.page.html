French Argentinean Workshop on Heterogeneous Catalysis

The Institute of Research on Catalysis and Petrochemistry, “Ing. José Miguel Parera“, INCAPE (CONICET - UNL), organizes the French Argentinean Workshop on Heterogeneous Catalysis. It will be held within the framework of an international cooperation project between the groups led by Dr. Florence Epron (Institut de Chimie des Milieux et Matériaux de Poitiers, IC2MP, France) and Dr. Carlos Pieck (INCAPE, Argentina): “International Research Proyect, OLECAT: Catalyseurs pour la production d’olefines”. The entire scientific community is invited to participate, from April 11 to 13, 2023 in Santa Fe, Argentina.


We invite you to discover more about the conference on this website, which will be regularly updated. We are looking forward to your participation!
