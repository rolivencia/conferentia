Developments, achievements and prospects on Heterogeneous Catalysis will be presented in plenary and keynote lectures by distinguished scientists. Oral communications and posters will also be included.

The event will be held mainly in the Auditorium of the Interdisciplinary Training Simulation Center of the Faculty of Medicine - National University of Littoral located in the University City adjoining the Scientific Technological Center Santa Fe site, where the Petunchi Building of INCAPE is located.

The French-Argentinean Workshop on Heterogeneous Catalysis was declared of interest by the Honorable Municipal Council of Santa Fe de la Vera Cruz on 18 August, 2022 (Declaration No. 5040), of institutional interest by the Board of Directors of the Faculty of Chemical Engineering (Resolution CD No. 423, 23 August, 2022) and also of institutional interest of the National University of Littoral (Resolution No. 5601, 9 September, 2022).

14 November 2022, the French Argentinean Workshop on Heterogeneous Catalysis was declared of interest to the Province of Santa Fe by Decree No. 2379.
